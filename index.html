Jiacheng Yang

博士研究生 / 计算机视觉、长尾学习、对抗性攻击 PHD Student / Computer Vision, Long-Tailed Learning, Adversarial attack

我目前在厦门大学攻读博士学位，导师为卢杨教授，硕士毕业于广东工业大学，导师为费伦科老师。我的研究方向主要包括长尾学习、图像生成与对抗性攻击等，目前已发表三篇相关论文。当前，我持续关注不平衡数据分布下的视觉学习方法、生成模型的鲁棒性与可控性，以及深度模型在安全性方面的关键问题。期待与对长尾视觉识别、生成式模型、对抗鲁棒性及相关交叉方向感兴趣的研究者开展交流与合作。

I am currently pursuing a Ph.D. at Xiamen University under the supervision of Prof. Yang Lu. I received my master’s degree from Guangdong University of Technology, where I was advised by Prof. Lunke Fei. My research interests include long-tailed learning, image generation, and adversarial attacks. So far, I have published three papers in these areas. My current work focuses on visual learning under imbalanced data distributions, the robustness and controllability of generative models, and security-related challenges in deep learning systems. I am always open to collaborations on long-tailed visual recognition, generative models, adversarial robustness, and related interdisciplinary topics.
